## 288. BLESSED BE THE NAME
####  Christ in Song,

O for a thousand tongues to sing:

3.
He breaks the pow'r of cancelled sin,
Blessed be the name of the Lord!
His blood can make the foulest clean,
Blessed be the name of the Lord!  [Chorus]
